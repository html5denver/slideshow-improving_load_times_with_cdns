What is a CDN?

Content Delivery Network

Distributing cloned web assets though multiple servers in different cities.

Internet at its best: fastest path!

  • Google
    • google-analytics
    • ajax.googleapis.com hosted libraries

CDN vs Plain
Old-School Hosting

  • Speed page loading
    • happy vistors = more $$$
    • reduce FOUT
    • better Google ranking
  • Better resistance to traffic/DoS spikes
  • Reduce hosting costs, I/O
  • Testing in online editors (JS Bin, CodePen, etc)

CDNs speed page loading

  • Faster hosting
    • Closer = fewer hops
    • Tuned to serve static files
  • Maxes out browsers' Max Connectoins

Browsers' HTTP Connections

(Originating Hostname ~ Max Connections via Browserscope)

  • FireFox : 6 of 17
  • Chrome : 6 of 10
  • IE11 : 13 of 17, IE10 : 8 of 17, IE8 & 9 : 6 of 35
  • Safari, iStuff : 6 of 17
  • Android 4 : 6 of 16, 2.3 : 8 of 10

FREE HTTP connections!

Max - Hostname = 2 to 11 hidden downloaders

  • Easy to use: replace static asset links with CDNs
    • Start with larger assets
    • Via Grunt/Gulp/build scripts
    • Wordpress plugins, etc
  • Workaround: 'shard' server
    • Subdomain: http://img.mysite.com/picture.jpg
    • May not help I/O, should test

Disadvantages

  • Could clog up other non-originating requests
    • Social plugins, tracking, API calls, ads...
  • Free CDNs can't use your personalized files

    • (excuses)

  • Drops cookies for tracking (advantage?)
  • DNS lookup tax
    • (only once & may be compensated by speed)
  • Bit extra time to set up
  • Downtime out of control your control
    • (not really; ~99.997% uptime)

Free CDN pit fight!

CloudFlare vs Google vs CDNJS vs jsDelivr

CloudFlare

  • Front-line security; all trafic hits CF first
  • agresssive content optimization
    • extra cruching for mobile
  • CDN growing; 27 POPs (2014-07-09)

    • CONS

  • Currently no POPs in China, Russia, Africa, Middle East
  • IPs get blocked for political content (China); cost of protecting free press

ajax.googleapis.com & google.com/fonts

  • Most popular
    • ~2% hitting cache
  • Maybe better for Africa, S America, some US cities
  • Good font selection

    • CONS

  • Slower in China
  • JS only for jQuery, AngularJS (users can't add more)

CDNJS

  • Uses CloudFlare's network & admins
    • ^ great resources
  • Optional auto-updates via NPM
  • Started earlier; more known than jsDelivr
  • Doesn't seem to be blocked when CloudFlare IPs are blocked

    • CONS

  • Smallest network, but growing
  • A bit slower than other Free CDNs

jsDelivr

  • CloudFlare + MaxCDN + 6 sponsored PoPs
    = 42 PoP MEGA-Network
    • Cedexis' load balancing helps make jsDelivr fastest
  • SemVer aliasing
  • Most projects (over 1130)
  • Collate JS | CSS assets
  • auto-updating bot

    • CONS

  • Newest; lesser known/used (but growing)

CDN Results

  • Faster loading = happier users
  • Happy users Free hosting = happier boss/client
  • YMMV: Test for yourself!
